1. Tic Tac Toe

Requirements
------------
Design a Tic Tac Toe where the board size can be nxn with 2 players

Ans:- 

Three classes
- Player 
    - Takes 2 input, first is the name of the player and the mark that wants to 
    be played as. For e.g 'x' or 'o'.

- Board 
    - Takes only 1 input that is the size of the board.

- Game
    - Takes 3 inputs. 
    - First 2 inputs are the player objects.
    - Last input is the board object.
